What is Exeum?

Guaranteed Stability

Fully backed by virtual assets. No need for overcollaterization. Free from black swan events and automatic liquidation.

Immediate Issuance

Pay in Exeum tokens and immediately receive stablecoins. No market fluctuations due to limited supply.

Low Fees

Zero hidden costs. No stability fees or dynamic transaction fees.

Team

Jaehyung Lee

Founder

Jaehyung was the co-founder and CTO of Enswers, acquired by Gracenote in 2015. He has 15+ years of industry experience with large-scale systems. He holds over 30 patents, including 22 in the US. Jaehyung received his BS in Computer Science and PhD in Bio and Brain Engineering from KAIST.

Minhyung Cho

Research

Minhyung was most recently the Principal Research Engineer at Gracenote. He has 10+ years of industry experience in applying machine learning techniques to production. He presented two geometric optimization papers on deep learning at NIPS in 2015 and 2017. He has worked on algorithmic trading software, and is passionate about AI-driven market making strategies. Minhyung holds a BS in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Seoul National University and a MS in Biosystems from KAIST.

Sherman Li

He was the co-founder of Enswers (acquired by Gracenote), CEO of Soompi (acquired by Crunchyroll), entrepreneur-in-residence at Softbank Ventures, co-founder of Evixar (acquired by LG).
